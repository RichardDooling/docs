# Discord

Buttondown's webhooks are _magical_. They automatically detect Discord and
format your newsletters to be sent as Discord messages:

![](/images/discord.png)

This means that it takes all of five seconds to start having notifications for new emails
or subscribers sent to your Discord server.

![](/images/discord-2.png)

Head on over to the [webhooks](/api-reference/events-and-webhooks) doc to learn more.
