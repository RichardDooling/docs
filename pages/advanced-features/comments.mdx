# Comments

## Frequently asked questions

### How do subscribers customize their name and profile photo?

The name and profile photo for a given email address is pulled from the active Buttondown account associated with that email address. If you'd like to change your name or profile photo, you can do so by [editing your account](https://buttondown.email/settings).
